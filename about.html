About

Basketball Standings – these standings are based on RPI which considers the teams’ winning percentage and strength of their opponents. This table considers both past opponents as well as future opponents when calculating RPI. This mirrors the official RPI rankings published by the district.

Past Game Standings – this table only considers games that have been played when calculating RPI. It is more reflective of the true rankings based on the games that have been played and doesn’t consider future opponents.

At the end of the season, both tables will be identical.

Tournament Bracket

  • Teams seeded 1-4 will automatically advance to A Bracket Round 2.
  • Teams seeded 13-16 will automatically move to B Bracket Round 2.
  • Teams seeded 5-12 will play in Round 1. Winners will advance to A Bracket Round 2 and losers will move to B Bracket Round 2.Teams seeded 17-18 will play each other in Round 1 but will not advance.
