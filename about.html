About

Basketball Standings – These standings use the RPI formula, which factors in each team's winning percentage and the strength of their opponents. Both past and future games are included in the calculation, mirroring the official RPI rankings published by the district.

Past Game Standings – This table calculates RPI using only games that have already been played. It provides a more accurate reflection of current team rankings, without considering future opponents.

At the end of the season, both tables will be identical since all games will have been played.

Tournament Bracket link

  • Teams seeded 1-4 will automatically advance to A Bracket Round 2.
  • Teams seeded 13-16 will automatically move to B Bracket Round 2.
  • Teams seeded 5-12 will play in Round 1. Winners will advance to A Bracket Round 2 and losers will move to B Bracket Round 2.Teams seeded 17-18 will play each other in Round 1 but will not advance.
